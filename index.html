My name is Ru-Yuan Zhang (张洳源 in Chinese). I am currently a postdoctoral research associate, working with Prof. Geoffery Ghose in Center for Magnetic Resonance Research at University of Minnesota Twin cities.

I got my Ph.D in 2016 from Department of Brain and cognitiveve Sciences and Center for Visual Science at University of Rochester (Rochester,NY). During my Ph.D, I worked with Prof. Daphne Bavelier and Prof. Duje Tadin. Super lucky! Two smart and nice advisors helped me a lot both in my life and academic career.

I was born in Zhangjiajie, a small city in middle south of China. It's famous for natural beauty. The memories with my parents and friends always make me homesick. From 2006, I majored in psychology and minored in computer science at Peking University, China. In 2007, Prof. Fang Fang offered me a research assistant position in his newly established lab. From then, I married to cognitive science. After my undergraduate study, I came to US to pursue my PhD study.
